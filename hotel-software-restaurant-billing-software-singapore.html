What is NINE DINE?

NINE DINE is wireless ordering POS (Point of sales) management system for restaurants. It wirelessly automates the ordering operations in the restaurants. Speedy ordering operation helps to increase the table turnover and sales.It also helps to improve the customer service and reduce the labor cost involved.
It manage all the details regarding POS like Customize order, Delivery of food, Serve to maximum Customer, Bills, Tax, etc.

NINE DINE manage the Fast wireless ordering operation, Remote prints of kitchen tickets, Delivery of food, Serve to maximum Customer, Bills, Tax, etc. That drive the growth of small and medium-sized Restaurant. We intend to provide our customers with the best POS & Ordering Management experience from beginning, with a smart Application. Easy-to-use, Accurate and secure order Management and fast delivery of food to customer.
