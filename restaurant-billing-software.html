Ice Cream POS

Are you running Ice Cream parlor? Facing problems in your business? Here you will get the complete solution for your Ice Cream parlor with NINE DINE. Restaurant POS

In restaurant POS you will get amazing features like Business Intelligence, KOT Delay, Dish availability and many more. You will get an email notification after closing your Restaurant which will include sales price, day end profit, inventory stock, Expenses of day. You can track your restaurant from anywhere. Using tablets you will save lots of money you spent on stationary.

Cafe POS

You will get a complete solution for café. You can able to track your total orders as well as table turnaround time (TOT), which helps you to serve more customers on a single table. You will be able to use features like billing management, order management, table management. KOT system will be handled automatically. You will have loyalty programs for your daily customers. You can give a discount in percentage as well as the flat amount. It will automatically calculate everything including your inventory and sales figures and gives you complete reports.
