Billing



Table Billing

Customer can pay Bill on the table itself. Customer can pay through various options like cash,card. Customer can split bill on same table, billing over item wise or payment mode type is provided.

Takeaway/Delivery Billing

We can take orders from web and mobile directly into point of sales system , so we can manage orders at one place and payments easily.

Mobile/Online Billing

Easily access the table order/dine-in/takeaway/delivery on the mobile. you can pay online also.convient to split bill and categorise bills

Multicurrency Support

support all the currencies in the world so it can be used anywhere worldwide.

Payment

Diffrent payment modes are avaiable. card payment, cash payment,paypal,etc

Accounting and Taxation

Automatic tax calculation provided also discount provided. once you assign % of tax or Discount ,it will calculate automatically.


Report and Analysis



Reporting and Analysis

Generates detailed reports so it helps to analyse your bussiness deeply. reports are provided in tabular form pie chart and according to date selection. Also generates report on perticular food item.

Graphical Analytics

Restaurant owner can get Bar chart for better graphical analytics. it helps alot to in decision making about your business.

Mobile Reporting

POS can generate Mobile Reports. Owner/Manager can get reports easily on his mobile

Offline Reporting

Offline reporting also provided. if internet connection gets down still POS works smoothly and uploads data when get connected to internet.
